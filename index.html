Delern Flashcards

Spaced repetition learning system. Ideal for studying. The application works on the principle of flashcards.

Features:

  • Synchronized across all devices;
  • Sharing with friend;
  • Background colors for german cards;
  • Available learning in offline;
  • Markdown support.

CoctailsPro

Are you preparing for a party or just want to cook something yummy for yourself? It means that this is the right app for you. Enjoy refreshing non-alcoholic drinks, explore and create different excellent cocktails, relish hot chocolate drinks. Add whatever you like to your favorites to have an offline access.

Features:

  • List of non-alcoholic beverages;
  • List of alcoholic cocktails;
  • List of Cocoa drinks;
  • List of Favourite drinks added by a user;
  • Detailed information about a drink: ingredients, preparation, picture.
